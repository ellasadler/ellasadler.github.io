About Me

I am from Ann Arbor, Michigan. I am 22 years old and am a senior at the University of Miami. I was once a Meteorology and Marine Science double major. When I was a sophomore I switched over to journalism due to my love of writing that I could no longer ignore.

I dream of writing my own TV series or novel one day. With a minor in Motion Pictures and a love for reading, this dream has developed in the past few years. I used to read a lot when I was much younger, but in high school I stopped due to my rigorous schedule. When quarantine hit, I decided to pick up books again.
