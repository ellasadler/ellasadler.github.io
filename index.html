Seasonal Book Recommendations

Books are for every season. Whether you are an aspiring reader or are just wanting to pick up a quick read, this website will help you navigate your next pick! Each of these reads are atmospheric and will transport you into a whole new world.

Each section of seasons has corresponding books that fit that seasons overall feel. You will be able to determine which best suits your mood and get yourself ready to hunker down with a new cozy read.

  • Summer Reads
  • Fall Reads
  • Winter Reads
  • Spring Reads

Summer Reads

What's more essential to summer than a beach read? This season is the most crucial time for cute romances and beachy tales that will have you beaming as bright as the sun!

  • Happy Place by Emily Henry
  • Malibu Rising by Taylor Jenkins Reid
  • The Fine Print by Lauren Asher
  • A Court of Thorns and Roses by Sarah J Maas
  • My Killer Vacation by Tessa Bailey
  • Love and Gelato by Jenna Evans Welch
  • Finlay Donovan Is Killing It by Elle Cosimano

Fall Reads

Fall is all about spooky season. It is also a time of reflection and change, making it the perfect time to read books that explore these ideas.

  • Red Rising by Pierce Brown
  • My Best Friend's Exorcism by Grady Hendrix
  • In My Dreams I Hold a Knife by Ashley Winstead
  • Indigo Ridge by Devney Perry
  • Kulti by Mariana Zapata
  • These Violent Delights by Chloe Gong
  • Ninth House by Leigh Bardugo

Winter Reads

As the sky gets darker and the days get colder, winter is the perfect time to curl up with a cozy read. Paired with a cup of hot chocolate and a warm blanket, these books will not disappoint!

  • In a Holidaze by Christina Lauren
  • Always, in December by Emily Stone
  • Migrations by Charlotte McConaghy
  • Legends and Lattes by Travis Baldree
  • Lore Olympus by Rachel Smythe
  • The Atlas Six by Olivie Blake
  • 11/22/63 by Stephen King

Spring Reads

Spring is a time of renewal and growth, making it the perfect season to read books that inspire and uplift. After a long, cold winter, these books will help motivate and encourage you. Spring has sprung!

  • Get a Life, Chloe Brown by Talia Hibbert
  • The Soulmate Equation by Christina Lauren
  • Pachinko by Min Jin Lee
  • Heartstopper by Alice Oseman
  • The Grace Year by Kim Liggett
  • Book Lovers by Emily Henry
  • The House in the Cerulean Sea by TJ Klune
